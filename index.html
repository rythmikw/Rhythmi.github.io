About Us

Hello Rhythmi's! We are a cutting-edge company dedicated to transforming the landscape of healthcare through the power of artificial intelligence. At Rhythmi, our mission is clear: to harness the capabilities of AI and data analytics to enhance the early detection and diagnosis of heart diseases. We are committed to revolutionizing the field of electrocardiography (ECG) by developing innovative AI models that not only assist in accurate ECG interpretation but also make it more accessible and user-friendly for healthcare professionals and patients alike. With a team of passionate experts in AI, cardiology, and data science, we strive to make a significant impact on the lives of millions by providing faster, more accurate, and more convenient tools for predicting and preventing heart diseases. Join us on our journey to create a healthier world through the harmony of technology and healthcare.
